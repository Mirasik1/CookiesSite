Our Special Recipe

Ingredients

  • 90g melted butter
  • 120g sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 250-300g flour
  • 100g chocolate

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 160 degrees Celsius.
  2. Mix the melted butter and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Beat in the egg to the mixture.
  4. Gradually add the flour and mix until the dough is consistent.
  5. Chop the chocolate into small pieces and fold them into the dough.
  6. Place spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.
  7. Bake for 17 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool before serving. Enjoy!
